About me.

After graduating with a degree in Sociology from the University of Nottingham in 2022, Soraya started exploring coding through Codecademy — just for fun at first. But that curiosity quickly turned into a genuine interest in programming and problem-solving.

In 2023, she decided to take things further and began an MSc in Computer Science at the University of York — a two-year conversion programme. Since then, her passion for development has continued to grow. She completed her final project in March 2025, and her graduation is set for the summer.
